## Corporate Overview
DOTMINI COMPANY LIMITED is a premier software engineering and digital infrastructure firm dedicated to building next-generation technology solutions. ## Proprietary Products & B2G Solutions
Internally developed infrastructure designed for specialized operations and government-level requirements.

### 1. MicroCode IDE
**The Next-Generation Native Development Environment**
MicroCode is our flagship, highly optimized Integrated Development Environment (IDE) engineered exclusively for the macOS ecosystem. Built utilizing a robust Rust backend and a strictly native SwiftUI frontend, MicroCode delivers unprecedented compilation and execution speed. It leverages advanced XPC Services and Mach IPC for deep OS-level integration, seamlessly incorporating enterprise-grade LLM protocols to accelerate the software development lifecycle.

### 2. Passadu +
**Governmental Asset & Supply Management Infrastructure (B2G)**
A highly secure, enterprise-grade management platform developed specifically for government agencies and large-scale public sector operations. Passadu + is architected to handle complex procurement pipelines, strict inventory tracking, and highly regulated logistical protocols, ensuring absolute compliance and operational transparency within the B2G sector.
