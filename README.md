## Corporate Overview
DOTMINI COMPANY LIMITED is a premier software engineering and digital infrastructure firm dedicated to building next-generation technology solutions. We operate at the intersection of low-level systems programming and advanced artificial intelligence, delivering mission-critical applications that demand uncompromising speed, security, and stability. Our engineering philosophy is strictly governed by a commitment to native execution—we categorically bypass cross-platform abstractions in favor of harnessing the full, bare-metal capability of the underlying hardware.

## Strategic Focus: B2B & B2G Integration
Our architectural frameworks are specifically engineered to serve the rigorous operational, compliance, and security demands of Business-to-Business (B2B) enterprises and Business-to-Government (B2G) agencies. 

* **Enterprise-Grade Security:** Implementing stringent data protection and secure execution environments suitable for sensitive corporate and government operations.
* **Scalable Infrastructure:** Building resilient backend architectures capable of processing complex, high-volume organizational workflows without degradation in system performance.
* **AI-Driven Automation:** Seamlessly integrating Large Language Model (LLM) agents to automate administrative protocols, enhance decision-making efficiency, and reduce operational overhead.

## Product Portfolio

### 1. MicroCode IDE
**The Next-Generation Native Development Environment**
MicroCode is a highly optimized, fully native Integrated Development Environment (IDE) engineered exclusively for the macOS ecosystem. Built from the ground up utilizing a robust Rust backend and a strictly native SwiftUI frontend, MicroCode delivers unprecedented compilation and execution speed. It leverages advanced XPC Services and Mach IPC to ensure deep OS-level integration, while seamlessly incorporating enterprise-grade LLM protocols to provide intelligent, context-aware development assistance.

### 2. Aeroteq
**Advanced Operational & Systems Management Platform**
Aeroteq is architected to provide structural oversight, telemetry, and precision management for complex corporate environments. Focused on high reliability and secure administrative control, Aeroteq streamlines internal processes and resource allocation, ensuring that large-scale enterprise and government operations function with maximum efficiency and absolute minimal downtime.

### 3. JBK Travel
**Corporate & Governmental Travel Resource Infrastructure**
A comprehensive logistical management and booking system tailored specifically for the complexities of B2B and B2G travel operations. JBK Travel centralizes organizational mobility, secure financial transaction handling, and strict travel policy compliance. It is engineered to coordinate multi-layered itineraries and logistical requirements seamlessly within a secure, closed-loop corporate ecosystem.

### 4. Custom Enterprise Implementations
**Bespoke Architecture & Deep System Integration**
Beyond our flagship software products, DOTMINI COMPANY LIMITED provides dedicated infrastructure engineering for highly specialized organizational needs. We deliver bespoke AI automation systems, robust database management interfaces, and low-level system integrations that plug directly into existing, large-scale governmental or corporate IT frameworks.
